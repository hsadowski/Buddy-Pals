About Us

Our Story

Buddy & Pal's Place has been a Crown Point institution since we first opened our doors in 1998. What started as a small neighborhood bar has grown into one of the Region's premier sports viewing destinations, while still maintaining our authentic local atmosphere.

Our founders, longtime friends who everyone called "Buddy" and "Pal," had a simple vision: create a place where friends could gather to enjoy great food, cold drinks, and watch the game together. That vision continues to guide everything we do today.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike corporate chain restaurants, we're deeply connected to our Crown Point community. We're proud to support local sports teams, host community events, and create a place where our staff knows the regulars by name.

We've designed our space with the sports fan in mind. Our strategic TV placement ensures there's not a bad seat in the house, and our audio zones let you hear the game you care about most.

Our Values

Quality Food

We believe bar food doesn't have to be ordinary. Our kitchen prepares scratch-made items daily using fresh ingredients, elevating classic sports bar fare to something special.

Community Focus

We're more than just a business - we're an active part of the Crown Point community. From sponsoring local teams to supporting community events, we're invested in our hometown.

Superior Viewing Experience

We've thoughtfully designed our space to provide the best possible sports viewing experience, with multiple TVs positioned for optimal viewing from any seat.

Personalized Service

Our staff takes pride in getting to know our regulars, remembering preferences, and creating a welcoming atmosphere for everyone who walks through our doors.
